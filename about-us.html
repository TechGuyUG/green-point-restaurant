Our Story

Discover the passion behind Green Point Restaurant, where authentic Ugandan flavors meet warm hospitality in the heart of Kampala.

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2007, Green Point Restaurant started as a small family-run eatery in Kampala with just four tables and a dream to share authentic Ugandan cuisine with our community. What began as a modest venture has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations, while still maintaining our family-oriented values.

Our founder, Sarah Nalwoga, grew up learning traditional cooking methods from her grandmother in Masaka. These cherished recipes, passed down through generations, form the foundation of our menu today.

"We don't just serve food - we serve memories. Every dish tells a story of our Ugandan heritage and the love we put into our cooking."

- Eunice Baluka, Founder & Head Chef

Our Culinary Philosophy

At Green Point, we believe in honoring Uganda's rich culinary traditions while embracing modern techniques that enhance flavors without compromising authenticity.

We source 90% of our ingredients from local farmers and markets, ensuring freshness while supporting our community. From the matooke in our Luwombo to the fresh fish from Lake Victoria, every ingredient is carefully selected for quality and sustainability.

Our chefs combine time-honored methods with creative presentations, offering both traditional favorites and innovative dishes that celebrate Uganda's diverse flavors.
